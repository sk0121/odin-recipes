Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

  • 1 package elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
    • 1 package shredded cheddar cheese, divided
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 2 egges, well beaten
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 canc ondensed Cheddar cheese soup
  • 1 pinch paprike

Instructions

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a roilling boil
  2. Stir in macaroni and return to a boil
  3. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite
  4. Drain and transfer pasta to a slow cooker
  5. Add butter to pasta and stir until melted; season with salt and pper
  6. Sprinke about half of the cheddar cheese over the pasta and stir
  7. Whisk evaporated milk and eggs together in a bowl until smooth;stir into pasta mixture
  8. Whisk milk and condensed soup together in a bowl until smooth; stir into pasta mixture
  9. Sprinkle remaining cheese over pasta mixture; garnish with paprika
  10. Cook on low for 3 hours and enjoy
