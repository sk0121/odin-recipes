Lasagna

Description

This recipe will teach you how to make Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Instructions

  • Make the meat sauce
  • Cook the noodles
  • Make the ricotta mixture
  • Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  • Cover the foil and bake
  • Let the lasagna rest before serving
